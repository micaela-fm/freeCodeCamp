Honey-Mustard Glazed Salmon

This honey-mustard glazed salmon is a quick and easy recipe that is perfect for a weeknight dinner. The sweet and tangy glaze pairs perfectly with the rich flavor of the salmon. Originally published on Taste of Home.

Ingredients

  • 10 salmon fillets (5 ounces each)
  • 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Place fillets, skin side down, on a greased baking sheet
  2. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, mustard, honey and salt; spoon over salmon.
  3. Broil 3-4 in. from the heat until fish just begins to flake easily with a fork, 8-12 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately.
